  • Grooming: cutting, washing, and coat care.

  • Training: positive reinforcement programs for dogs and cats.
  • Nutrition: food selection and dietary recommendations.
  • Dog walking and pet-sitting services.
How often should I take my pet for grooming?
The frequency of grooming depends on the breed and condition of your pet's coat. Generally, it is recommended:
  • Short-haired breeds: every 2-3 months.
  • Long-haired breeds: every 4-6 weeks.
  • Animals with skin problems may require more frequent grooming.
